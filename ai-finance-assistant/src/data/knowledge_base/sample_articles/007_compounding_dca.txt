Dollar-cost averaging (DCA) is investing a fixed amount on a regular schedule (e.g., monthly). DCA can reduce the impact of buying all at once at a market peak, and it helps build consistent habits.

Important note: DCA does not guarantee profit, but it can reduce timing anxiety and create disciplined progress toward a goal.
